## Part 0 · Orientation

### The Two Books at a Glance

These are companion texts — written the same year, drawing on overlapping research — yet they feel like different people explaining the same mountain from opposite slopes. Wyner stands at the base and tells you exactly what to pack. Nation stands at the top and tells you why each path leads where it does.
