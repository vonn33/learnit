## A3 · Key Researcher Reference

<DataTable
  headers={["Researcher & Work", "Contribution"]}
  rows={[
    ["Ebbinghaus (1885)", "Forgetting curve and spacing effect. Spaced repetition is dramatically more efficient than massed study."],
    ["Hebb (1949)", "'Neurons that fire together, wire together.' Multi-sensory encoding creates stronger memories by co-activating multiple neural circuits."],
    ["Craik & Lockhart (1972)", "Levels of processing. Deeper semantic and self-referential encoding produces more durable memories than shallow structural encoding."],
    ["Krashen (1982)", "Comprehensible input (i+1) and Affective Filter. Influential but contested; the acquisition-learning distinction is now considered oversimplified."],
    ["Swain (1985)", "Output Hypothesis. Production forces syntactic commitment that comprehension avoids. Immersion input alone is insufficient for accurate grammar."],
    ["Schmidt (1990)", "Noticing Hypothesis. Conscious attention to a linguistic form is necessary (or strongly accelerating) for acquisition."],
    ["Anderson / DeKeyser (1983–2015)", "Skill Acquisition Theory. Knowing ≠ doing fluently. Automaticity requires dedicated speed practice — distinct from comprehension or study."],
    ["Roediger & Karpicke (2006)", "Testing effect. Retrieval practice produces 2× better 1-week retention than repeated study (80% vs. 36%)."],
    ["Nation (2001, 2014)", "Vocabulary coverage thresholds; four-strand framework; learning conditions; interference effects. The empirical backbone of applied vocabulary teaching."],
    ["Bialystok et al. (2007+)", "Bilingualism delays dementia onset 4–5 years. Executive function and hippocampal maintenance advantages confirmed by neuroimaging (2024)."],
    ["Deci & Ryan (SDT)", "Autonomous motivation predicts L2 achievement and persistence better than any specific technique. Three needs: autonomy, competence, relatedness."],
  ]}
